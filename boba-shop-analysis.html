Boba Shop Sales Performance Analysis

📅 Completed: February 2026 🏢 Industry: Food & Beverage ⏱️ Duration: 6 months data

Conducted comprehensive sales performance analysis for a boba tea shop using 6 months of transactional data (49,000+ orders). Identified peak sales periods, top-performing products, customer ordering patterns, and revenue optimization opportunities. Analysis revealed actionable insights for inventory management, staffing optimization, and menu engineering to improve operational efficiency and profitability.

Business Objective

  • Optimize inventory management based on product demand patterns
  • Identify peak sales periods for staffing optimization
  • Analyze menu performance to guide pricing and promotion strategies
  • Understand customer preferences across order channels
  • Improve operational efficiency and reduce waste

Analytical Approach

  • Time-series analysis of daily and hourly sales patterns
  • Product performance ranking by revenue and volume
  • Channel analysis (In-Store, UberEats, DoorDash, Grubhub)
  • Customer behavior analysis (size preferences, toppings, customization)
  • Revenue optimization and menu engineering recommendations

Key Findings

  • Peak hours identified: 2-4 PM and 6-8 PM drive 60% of daily revenue
  • Top 20% of menu items generate 65% of total revenue
  • Delivery channels (UberEats, DoorDash) represent 45% of orders
  • Thursday-Saturday account for 55% of weekly sales
  • Premium toppings (Cheese Foam, Boba Pearls) increase average ticket by 25%

Business Impact

  • Staffing recommendations: Add 2 staff during peak hours (projected 15% efficiency gain)
  • Inventory optimization: Focus stock on top 30 performers (reduce waste by 20%)
  • Menu engineering: Promote high-margin items, discontinue 15 low performers
  • Channel strategy: Optimize delivery platform presence during peak hours
  • Pricing strategy: Premium topping bundling projected to increase AOV by 12%
