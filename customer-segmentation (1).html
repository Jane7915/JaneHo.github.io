Client Challenge

  • Customer acquisition costs reaching $45 per customer
  • Undifferentiated marketing approach across customer base
  • Limited visibility into customer value distribution
  • Constrained marketing budget requiring optimization
  • No systematic identification of churn risk indicators

Analytical Approach

  • RFM framework (Recency, Frequency, Monetary value)
  • K-means clustering with optimal validation (k=4)
  • Statistical segmentation and behavioral profiling
  • Revenue concentration and CLV analysis
  • Strategic recommendations by segment

Strategic Insights

  • High-value segment (12% of base) drives 53% of revenue
  • Core loyal segment (39%) represents expansion opportunity
  • At-risk cohort (29%) requires immediate intervention
  • Dormant high-spenders (20%) present reactivation potential
  • Clear behavioral patterns enable predictive modeling

Business Outcomes

  • Segment-specific retention and growth strategies
  • 20% projected increase in customer lifetime value
  • Marketing budget reallocation framework (40% to VIPs)
  • 30% improvement in marketing efficiency and ROI
  • Operational playbook for ongoing segmentation
