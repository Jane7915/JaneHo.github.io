Seattle Japanese Food — Social Media Strategy Analysis

📅 Completed: March 2026 🏢 Industry: Food & Beverage / Digital Marketing 🎯 Focus: Content Strategy & Platform Analysis

Business Problem

A Seattle-based Japanese food brand needed to understand which content topics, keywords, and social media platforms would generate the highest reach and engagement before investing in a content marketing strategy. Key unknowns included which keywords had the best views-to-engagement ratio, how their YouTube performance compared to industry benchmarks, and whether search interest in ramen content was growing or declining heading into 2026.

Collected and analyzed real YouTube API data alongside simulated platform data (Instagram, TikTok, X) to build a two-dashboard content strategy framework. Identified ramen mukbang as the highest-opportunity keyword at 23M average views, confirmed YouTube performance at 145× above the industry benchmark, and tracked rising Google Trends interest in ramen core content through early 2026. Delivered platform-level virality analysis and keyword-level opportunity scoring across 13 tracked search terms.

Project Objectives

  • Identify highest-opportunity keywords for YouTube content targeting Seattle Japanese food
  • Compare real YouTube channel performance against Dash Social industry benchmarks
  • Analyze virality patterns across YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and X by topic category
  • Track Google Trends search interest over time for ramen core vs side dish content
  • Deliver actionable platform and content recommendations backed by data

Analytical Approach

  • YouTube Data API v3 extraction: 317 real videos across 13 keyword searches
  • Google Trends API: Weekly search interest data from Nov 2023 to Mar 2026
  • Kaggle synthetic dataset: 2,000 simulated posts across Instagram, TikTok, X
  • Benchmark comparison using Dash Social 2024 industry averages
  • Keyword scoring by avg views, avg likes, engagement rate, and trends momentum
  • Two-dashboard Tableau build: industry overview + content strategy

Key Findings

  • Ramen mukbang drives 23.3M avg views — 2× the next highest keyword, confirming mass-appeal content dominates niche Seattle terms
  • YouTube channel performs 145× above the Dash Social industry benchmark for avg video views (9.9M vs 68K)
  • Ramen Core search interest shows a consistent upward trend from 2024 through early 2026 — growing opportunity window
  • Seattle-specific keywords (Seattle izakaya, ramen Seattle review) show very low reach but strong like-to-view ratios, indicating a loyal niche audience
  • All platforms show similar viral rates (~69–71%) — content quality matters more than platform choice
  • X (Twitter) leads engagement rate at 20.1% but has a declining user base — Instagram and TikTok offer more sustainable growth

Recommendations & Impact

  • Primary content focus: Ramen mukbang and ramen 2025 formats — highest reach, proven audience demand
  • Platform priority: Instagram and TikTok for growth; YouTube for depth and search discoverability
  • Seattle-specific content strategy: Use as community-building layer, not primary growth driver — volume is low but loyalty is high
  • Timing opportunity: Publish ramen core content now — Google Trends shows momentum accelerating into 2026
  • Side dish keywords (takoyaki, gyoza) represent a mid-tier opportunity with lower competition and moderate views
  • Data note: Instagram/TikTok/X findings based on Kaggle simulation — validate with real platform data before final budget allocation
